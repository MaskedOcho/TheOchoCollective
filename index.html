Who We Are
08

The Ocho Collective is a small studio built on one idea: what we make for you should be built for you, not pulled off a template — whether that's a website, a CAD model, or a printed part.

We work with small businesses and individuals — bakeries, coaches, repair shops, clinics, makers — who need something that actually does the job: a site that takes orders, a part that fits right the first time, a model that's ready to print or manufacture.

Every project runs through the same three-part process — build, develop, deliver — so you always know what stage you're at and what's coming next.
